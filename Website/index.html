Durham House of Pets is one of the oldest operating pet stores in the country. Our founder John Smith sold pieces of rope to Native Americans to lease the first wolf pups they were trying to domesticate. From there the pet store grew with the United States into the industrial revolution. The pet store took off during this time. With the invention of the assembly line the pet store could produce mass numbers of rubber balls that were given to dogs to play fetch. As iron became cheaper the pet store could go into the bird cage business. this allowed the company to expanded to include bird lovers. Our company weathered the first and second world wars by making bird cages out of plastic when iron became increasingly expensive. The long history brings us to today. We are a small company with values tied to this wonderful city and want to watch it grow and change as we have. So, have a look around and see what this store can offer you and your pets. Enjoy!
