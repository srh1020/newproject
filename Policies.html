Financing-

No intrest if paid in full in 6 months *on storewide purchases $199 and up. Interest will be charged to your account from the purchase date if the purchase balance is not paid in full within 6 months.

Delivery-

Delivery starts at $29! Prices range from $29 to $59. The prices vary based on demand and distance from the closest store to your shipping address.

Return Policies-

Merchandise purchased online can be returned via mail or returned in-person to any of our store locations (please see the address on the Contact us page). Please keep in mind that in-store returns will be issued store credit that can only be used in-store. No in-person drop offs will be accepted at our Distribution Center or Corporate Office. We recommend shipping packages back with a tracking number, lost packages without proof of receipt will not be given return credit.
